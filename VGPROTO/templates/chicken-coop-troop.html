Chicken Coop Troop

Chicken Coop Troop is a game I've been planning on making for a while. When my prototyping class came up, and I saw the assignment, I dicided to make this as a prototype instead. you can try it out for yourself, and see what came of a 5 somthing year old scribble in a notebook when the right motivation drove me to pressue it. This project was completed in about 2 weeks with me and Stewart Holmes and I'm quite happy where it ended.

From the start, right to the end the project went well, the best of all the first quarter including the little side things. We made a plan, and a time table, and stuck to it. I think the amount of polish really shows in the details.
